# Code of conduct

CodeSync contributors and maintainers are expected to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.
Harassment, discrimination, threats, publication of another person's private information, and
deliberate disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate,
documented privately where appropriate, and applied consistently.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method listed on the
[maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/kaustubh-dot). Do not include credentials or other
sensitive data in a public issue.
